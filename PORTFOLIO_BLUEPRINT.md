# Portfolio Blueprint

## Positioning
- Position the site as a modern software engineer building thoughtful, high-trust products at the intersection of product design, frontend craft, and AI-native systems.
- Emphasize calm confidence over hype and make the experience feel premium, minimal, and easy to scan.

## Core Pages
- Home
- About
- Projects
- Experience
- Blog
- Contact

## SEO Direction
- Primary keyword: software engineer portfolio
- Secondary keywords: Next.js developer, AI product engineer, product engineering portfolio
- Suggested title template: Pratham Gosai | Software Engineer

## Content Strategy
- Use concise hero messaging, proof-driven sections, and a curated project archive.
- Publish short, high-signal articles that demonstrate perspective and expertise.
- Link back to the portfolio from GitHub and LinkedIn whenever new content is published.

## Conversion Strategy
- Make the contact CTA highly visible and low-friction.
- Use trust-building sections and specific project outcomes to make the site feel credible.
- Keep the navigation simple and focused on the work.

## Technical Direction
- Next.js app router with TypeScript
- Tailwind-based design system
- Reusable UI components and lightweight motion
- Strong metadata and accessible structure
